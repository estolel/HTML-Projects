My Top 5 Favorite Movies

Mobile Suit Gundam: Hathaway

  • It is a movie about a very talented pilot trying to push his ideals on mankind living in Earth. Many disagree however, as they think his ideas are flawed.

    • Shin Godzilla

  • A mysterious monster emerges from Tokyo Bay and wreaks havoc upon Japan. A very beautiful iteration in the Godzilla series. It is a great parallelization for the criticism of the Japanese government's response to the Fukushima disaster.

    • We Were Soldiers

  • The story of the first major battle of the American phase of the Vietnam War, and the soldiers on both sides that fought it, while their wives wait nervously and anxiously at home for the good news or the bad news. An amazing and very realistic war movie.

    • No Game No Life: Zero

  • A movie set thousands of years before the main series, this movie is a really emotional trip involving very likeable characters that we both know and don't know.

    • Rascal Does Not Dream of a Dreaming Girl

  • A sequel movie to the anime "Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai", this movie is very good at using time as a narrative tool. An emotional rollercoaster and a must watch if you've watched the original anime aired in 2018
